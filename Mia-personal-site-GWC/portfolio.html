Let's Keep Colleges

For Kode with Klossy, we were instructed to make a website that could help create change. My group decided to create a website that informed people of the effects COVID-19 has had on colleges and universities. To do so, we looked up statistics and analyzed data to give a sense of the impact, and also linked petitions and places for people to donate.

Personality Quiz

For the SIP with Girls who Code in 2020, we were instructed to create a Buzzfeed-inspired quiz. For my project, I decided to create a quiz that classify a participant as one of the four seasons based on their responses to questions that were asked. This quiz has five questions, each with four answer choices, and 5 possible results can be given (winter, spring, summer, fall, or unclear).
